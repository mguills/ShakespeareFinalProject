 The hunt up, moon bright gray, The fields fragrant, woods green. Uncouple here, let us make bay And wake Emperor lovely bride, And rouse Prince, ring hunter's peal, That court may echo noise. Sons, let charge, ours, To attend Emperor's person carefully. I troubled sleep night, But dawning day new comfort hath inspired. Many good morrows Majesty;-- Madam, many, good.-- I promised Grace hunter's peal. And I horse follow game Makes way runs like swallows o'er plain. I did, lord, yet let bail, For father's reverend tomb I vow They shall ready Highness' To answer suspicion lives.
